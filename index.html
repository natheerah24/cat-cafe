About

aCquAinTance Cat Cafe, which opened in mid-2022, is Africa's second Cat Cafe. It is a safe and happy home for 32 rescued non-breed cats. As a non-profit organization, our goal is to raise awareness about shelter cats and to help eliminate pet homelessness through education about rescue and spay/neuter services. aCquAinTance invites you to enjoy the physical and emotional benefits of spending time with its residents while maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness. You can feed our kitties their favorite food at snack time while indulging in a variety of treats from our modest kitchen.
